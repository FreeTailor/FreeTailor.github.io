Methodology

We propose FreeTailor, a tuning-free method that achieves trajectory-controllable video generation by leveraging the noise-generation dependency. Specifically, we comprehensively delve into the 3D spatio-temporal distribution in initial noise and reveal that the spatio-temporal anomalous noise regions can more effectively control motion trajectory of generated content in various trajectories.


With this insight, we further design the spatio-temporal anomalous regions based on variance deviation and noise reuse, and then utilize them to achieve initial noise customization for trajectory-controllable video generation. Additionally, we utilize minimal attention modifications to prevent the influence of anomalous regions from leaking into the background.
